DESIGN & BUILD-OUT

RobinSport makes your brand shine

When talk about design and build-out begin, budget concerns follow shortly thereafter. What is our take on pricing? We charge the rack rate for every third second followed by… just kidding. That kind of pricing doesn't exist at RobinSport. Instead, we keep it simple and affordable. How?

First, we purchased our building about an hour outside of downtown Chicago. Why? Honestly, we got a great deal on a very modern building that will allow us to grow into over the years to come.

Two, we don't like to ask our partners to help us pay for our lavish downtown offices with a fountain in the waiting room. Look, we all know that the bigger the agency, the nicer the office, the more it will cost our partners to do business with us.

Third, we don't have a CEO, COO, CFO or any other C Suite titles that require large salaries and even larger bonuses. Instead, we have a great leader and founder who invest in his faith, his community and his people. We have found that our pricing philosophy works very well for our partners, both big and small.

It is important to note though that if a budget for a project is not in proper order for us to provide quality work, we will most likely pass on the opportunity. Our job is to make your brand shine, not cut corners and find ways to "just get it done."

We work with some of the world’s most famous brands.

We’ve created events for Red Bull, Jordan Brand (Nike), Harley-Davidson, Suzuki, Mercedes-Benz, Ferrari, Fox Entertainment, McDonald's, Sprite, and Bacardi. However, we've learned that our strength is in execution.

RobinSport is a leading experiential marketing agency. We activate brands using contemporary content marketing techniques which embrace strategy, branding, graphic design, web design, public relations, corporate communication, digital, marketing and advertising.
