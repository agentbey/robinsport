TEAM

So, who owns this place? What makes him tick?

Kreig Robinson has been at the helm since 2006. He started Robinsport because he has great passion for live events. He has an even greater passion for helping our partners build unique, one of a kind experiences, that create strong consumer emotional connections with their brands.

Kreig learned a lot about creating emotional connections when he worked at Red Bull North America. He learned a lot about strategy and logistics when he was a VP at Michael Jordan Motorsports.

And like every great leader, he surrounds himself with incredible people who have similar passions and experiences with major brands. Oh, did I mention that Kreig comes to the office everyday? That he goes to most of the events to help set-up, execute and tear-down?

You will not find Kreig behind some fancy, expensive, desk or in some remote luxury resort leaving his team to run the company. He prefers to be hands on with his team, showing them that true craftsmanship still exist in today's churn-in - churn - out consumer facing event industry.
