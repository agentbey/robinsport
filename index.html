STRATEGY & PLANNING

At RobinSport, we listen. And then we listen more. Finally, we listen even more. We listen to our partners to understand what their goals are for each event.

We do not take a cookie cutter approach as each event and idea are unique to our partners. We leverage our expertise in Live Events and offer ideas and suggestions that have proven success.

DESIGN & BUILD-OUT

We build and fabricate display units from the ground-up, print and install graphics, offer custom paint services, provide trucks, trailers, drivers, and manage the overall activation of the program.

We have amazing capabilities but if for some reason we have to farm out a service, it is always a pass through to our partners. We do not tack on 20% as we don't feel that it is the right thing to do. That is just part of our transparency at RobinSport.

EXECUTION & MANAGEMENT

RobinSport creates, maintains, services, and manages specialty vehicles for clients requiring vivid, visually dynamic and immersive consumer engagement programs. We train the drivers, handle the payroll, schedule the stops, and Armor All the tires. We're often the face of our clients brands, on the move.
