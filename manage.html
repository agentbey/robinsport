Execution & Management

high levels of engaging experience

We manage the storage, maintenance, and national deployment of your event infrastructure assets and premiums. RobinSport supports event marketing activations by providing specialized staff to help execute events.

Our trained ambassadors are integrated into brands as if they were internal, providing high levels of engaged experience at events. We focus on quick set-up times, rapid deployment and diverse activation sites within a market.

RobinSport manages the fleets of clients who provide experiences either on a short-term or mid-term basis. This often includes performing scheduled maintenance, light to medium repairs or part replacement, fleet scheduling, billing, logistics and storage.

Some projects require long term management. RobinSport places contract professionals internally to manage all aspects of marketing and advertising campaigns from an execution standpoint.
