Who is Robinsport?

Why don't we start with who we are not.

We are NOT a full service marketing agency. Why? Well, it's by design. We believe that trying to be everything to everyone results in sub-par work. Think of it this way; world renowned glass artist, Dale Chihuly, focuses on creating amazing, beautiful works of art out of glass.

He doesn't spend time on metals, plastics, paper or other materials. If he did, he might begin to lose his edge working with glass. At RobinSport, we focus only in one area of marketing, LIVE EVENTS. It is what we have great passion for. It is what we have great experience in.

Why don't we just hire several experts in all the areas of marketing and become a full service agency? You know, like all the other big players? We at RobinSport call that unnecessary overhead. When a company "grows" to look like the others, only to compete, they are destined to fail.

Don't get us wrong, we love our employees and our growth over the years but we prefer to stay financially strong and focus on long-term, steady growth in our area of expertise.

So, who are we then?
