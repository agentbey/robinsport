White glove service

We can manage the storage, maintenance, and national deployment of your event infrastructure assets and premiums. Traditional or electronic, our team handles your items with white glove service.

Mobile Display Vehicles and Touring Advertising Trucks

RobinSport creates, maintains, services, and manages specialty vehicles for clients requiring vivid, visually dynamic and immersive consumer engagement programs. We train the drivers, handle the payroll, schedule the stops, and Armor All the tires. We're often the face of our clients brands, on the move.

Fleet Management

RobinSport manages the fleets of clients who provide experiences either on a short-term or mid-term basis. This often includes performing scheduled maintenance, light to medium repairs or part replacement, fleet scheduling, billing, logistics and storage.

Asset Management

A lot like Fleet Management, RobinSport manages and maintains the event infrastructure of clients. Whether it's a multi-million dollar trade show display or the inventory of tchotskes to be launched at a concert, we make sure it's ready to go for our client's needs.

Event Staffing

RobinSport supports event marketing activations by providing specialized staff to help execute events. Our trained ambassadors are integrated into brands as if they were internal, providing high levels of engaged experience at events.

Additionally, our staff builds custom displays for events incorporating sound, lighting, and crowd control features. We focus on quick set-up times, rapid deployment and diverse activation sites within a market.

Contract Staffing

Some projects require long term management. RobinSport places contract professionals internally to manage all aspects of marketing and advertising campaigns from an execution standpoint.
