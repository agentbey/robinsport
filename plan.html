STRATEGY & PLANNING

Only have an idea and need help shaping it into a vision?

Or, have the vision and need assistance in planning it out? Maybe you just have what you need to achieve and need a trusted partner to create the strategy, design and execute it? We are here to help at any level.

We've developed strategy, provided design concepts, managed executions and just lent a helping hand for our partners such as Harley-Davidson, Red Bull North America, Mike’s Harder Lemonade, Michael Jordan Motorsports and others

Why do we keep saying partners and not client or customer? Answer - In order for us to succeed, we have to be partners when we work together. We have to be transparent, candid and ego-less. Our job is to help you succeed. Our job is not to find ways to sell you things you don't need and have our favorite phrase be "change-order."

In addition, we are very blessed, in part due to our company structure, that we do not need to take on every job that comes our way in order to keep the lights on. We are selective with who we partner with so that we can spend the right amount of time needed to build on a long-term relationship.

RobinSport is a leading experiential marketing agency. We activate brands using contemporary content marketing techniques which embrace strategy, branding, graphic design, web design, public relations, corporate communication, digital, marketing and advertising.
